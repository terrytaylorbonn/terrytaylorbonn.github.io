This section will focus on **rapidly spinning up practical real-world AI projects** with minimal manual coding ("spin up", not “vibe", coding). The focus will be higher level than in section [(5) AI dev tools](/AI-dev-tools/).

The specific business cases will come from a project/product manager perspective.  For example:

- **Project risk radar**. Reads status updates, Jira/Linear tickets, Slack summaries, and milestones to flag blockers, slipping dates, missing owners, or vague dependencies.
- **Competitive intelligence brief generator**. Summarizes competitor websites, release notes, pricing pages, job posts, and news into weekly product or market briefs.
- **Document intake and extraction**. Extracts structured data from contracts, invoices, applications, forms, PDFs, or emails and routes it into a CRM, spreadsheet, database, or workflow tool.
