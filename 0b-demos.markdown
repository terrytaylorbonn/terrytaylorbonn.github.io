This page will be a list of demos. Basically a start to finish AI/agents course.

I often see Facebook ads about learning AI. They promise you will **understand AI in 30 days spending only 15 minutes a day studying** (some courses are even better; they only require 10 minutes a day). I ain't smart enough to create such a course. Or a course that demos the "intelligence" of AI. My demos take time and demo how AI is a non-intelligent set of extremely useful tools. My approach is **mechanistic**, because that's what AI tools are. **Binary, deterministic, mechanistic.**

My original Wiki goal was to create such a start to finish demo series. But I kept switching gears, often changing focus, reorganizing everything. In any case
